1.2 Location Data

Our App collects and processes location data to:

  • Show real-time bus locations at a specific bus stop.
  • Track buses for arrival time estimates.
  • Provide navigation assistance to nearby bus stops.
  • Determine the nearest buses and stops to your location.

Location data collection occurs in these scenarios:

  • When you explicitly enable location services for the App.
  • When you use the bus tracking feature and mark yourself as being inside a bus.
  • When you search for nearby buses or stops.
